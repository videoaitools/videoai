1. Overview

Saasutility is an informational landing experience that may include links to third-party platforms or services. This policy covers information collected on this website and how it is used.

If you contact us or submit a form (if enabled), we may collect details like your name, email, and message content.

2.2 Automatic data (usage & device)

When you visit the site, we may automatically collect basic technical information such as IP address, browser type, device identifiers, pages viewed, and time spent, for analytics and security.

2.3 Third-party links

Our site may include outbound links. If you click to a third-party site, their privacy practices apply.

3. How We Use Information

We may use cookies or similar technologies to remember preferences, measure performance, and support marketing attribution. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may impact certain site features.

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with:

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or object to certain processing.

You can also control cookies via browser settings and opt-out mechanisms where available.

8. Children’s Privacy

This website is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
